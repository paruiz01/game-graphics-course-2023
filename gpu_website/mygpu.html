GPUs on devices

  • Phone: iPhone SE 2 Apple Integrated GPU (4-core graphics)
  • Tablet: iPad Pro 2022 Apple Integrated GPU (10-core GPU)
  • PC: Dedicated GTX 980 Ti
  • Gaming Console: PS5 Integrated AMD Oberon Console GPU

    • What kind of Graphic API's and Shading Languages do they support?

  • Phone: Metal and has its own shading language called Metal Shading Language (MSL).
  • Tablet: Metal and has its own shading language called Metal Shading Language (MSL).
  • PC: The GTX980 Ti has 3 graphics APIs which are OpenGL, DirectX and Vulkan. It supports HLSL, GLSL, Cg and CUDA for shading languages.
  • Gaming Console: The PS5 supports the GNM and GNMX graphic API's. Sony has their own shading language called PSSL(PlayStation Shader Language).
